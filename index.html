DM - Cardiology,

Interventional Cardiologist

Dr. Nagesh Waghmare is an accomplished Interventional Cardiologist based in Mumbai, India, with extensive academic and professional credentials. Holding a distinguished background, he completed his MBBS from Grant Medical College, Mumbai, followed by an MD in General Medicine from Seth G.S. Medical College & K.E.M. Hospital.

WHAT WE DO

AT HEAL-THYHEART CLINIC

Our mission at Heal-Thy-Heart Cardiology is to prevent, diagnose, and treat cardiac conditions as well as heart rhythm issues through a range of
diagnostic methods and treatments. We seek to provide patients compassionate and personalized care as well as to give each patient the individual
attention they need.

ADVANCED DIAGNOSTIC STUDIES

Ensuring accurate diagnosis and early treatment of heart conditions

CUTTING EDGE TREATMENTS

Customized treatments conservative & surgical options to restore heart health.

PREVENTING HEART DISEASE

Ensuring heart health by helping identify and avoid risk factors.

PERSONALIZED & COLLABORATIVE SOLUTIONS

Multidisciplinary patient-centered approach which includes good diet, exercise coaching & cardiac rehab.
